A Brand New RPG for the Nintendo DS

Prepare for a Very Slimey Journey Into the Depths of Hell!

Into hell is a brand new Platformer RPG developed for the Nintendo DS. The very first project undertaken by Proserpina Collabs, Into Hell aims to capture the essence of portable gaming of the mid-2000s whilst combining it with modern design theory, innovative gameplay and more!

The player will embark on a journey from the comfort of our realm into the twisted pits of Hell, collecting items, meeting characters and battling fierce enemies! Watch as the landscape around you takes a turn for the worst as you progress deeper Into Hell!
